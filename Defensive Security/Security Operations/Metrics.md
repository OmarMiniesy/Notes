### Metrics

- Alerts Count - AC - Total Count of Alerts Received / Overall load of SOC analysts
- False Positive Rate -	FPR - False Positives / Total Alerts	Level of noise in the alerts
- Alert Escalation Rate	- AER - Escalated Alerts / Total Alerts	Experience of L1 analysts
- Threat Detection Rate	- TDR - Detected Threats / Total Threats	Reliability of the SOC team

### SLAs - Service Level Agreements

These are signed agreements between the SOC and the customer that ensure quick detection & remediation of threats.
- SOC Availability - Working schedule of the SOC team.
- Mean Time to Detect - Average time between attack and its detection by SOC tools.
- Mean Time to Acknowledge - Average time for L1 analysts to start triage of a new alert.
- Mean Time to Respond - Average time taken by SOC to stop the breach from spreading.
